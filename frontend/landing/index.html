About us

Built on discipline, guided by wisdom StoicronApp was created to help people work with clarity, purpose, and emotional balance in a world full of distractions. We believe productivity is not about doing more—it’s about doing what matters, with intention. That’s why we combined the Pomodoro technique, a proven method for focus and time management, with Stoic philosophy, a timeless framework for resilience and inner calm. Stoicism teaches us to focus on what we can control. Pomodoro helps us do exactly that—with our time.
